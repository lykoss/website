Rules

These are the current rules for ##werewolf.
Breaking these rules may lead to a stasis (prevents you from joining games) or a ban.

Channel rules

  • Use common sense.
  • Be nice to each other. No racism or homophobia. Seriously, don't rage at each other.
  • Keep the channel family-friendly / safe for work.
  • Unauthorized bots, public logging and/or related activities are not permitted.
  • Automated scripts, such as away notifications, are disallowed.
  • Don't spam. This includes, but is not limited to:
    • Manually highlighting people.
    • Spamming and/or abusing !start or other bot commands.
    • Posting text longer than 3 lines. Use a pastebin like bpaste.net or pastebin.com.
    • Posting ASCII art. If you really have to, use a pastebin or link to a picture.
    • Posting garbage or other worthless messages.
    • Excessive shouting (CAPS LOCK). It's equally bad if your Shift key is hanging.
    • Abusing colours.

Gameplay rules

  • Don't share information after dying.
  • Don't play with clones. Any player found to be using clones will immediately be banned from the game.
  • Don't post private messages or screenshots of private messages from the bot during the game, regardless whether real or not.
  • Don't use /whois idle times.
  • Don't !quit or !leave after a game has started unless you really need to leave.
  • Don't use anti-idle scripts.
